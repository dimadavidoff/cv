Wing Tsun (also spelled as Wing Chun or Ving Tsun) is a traditional Chinese martial art and self-defense system that focuses on close-range combat and practical techniques. It is known for its efficiency and directness, making it a popular choice for self-defense and martial arts enthusiasts. Wing Tsun has several different lineages and variations, each with its own specific techniques and training methods. Some of the most well-known Wing Tsun lineages include the Yip Man (Ip Man) lineage and the Leung Ting lineage. Over the years, Wing Tsun has gained popularity worldwide, and it is practiced both for self-defense and as a martial art. It has also been popularized in movies and popular culture, notably through films featuring Bruce Lee and Donnie Yen.
